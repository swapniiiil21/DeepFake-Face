Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about deepfake detection

How accurate is your deepfake detector?

Our detector achieves approximately 92-95% accuracy on benchmark datasets. However, no detector is perfect, especially as deepfake technology improves. We recommend using our tool as one of several verification methods.

Do you store the images I upload?

No, we do not store your images permanently. Images are processed temporarily for analysis and then deleted. For maximum privacy, we're working on a client-side version that processes images entirely in your browser.

What types of deepfakes can you detect?

Our detector is trained to identify various types of AI-generated faces, including those created by GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks) and diffusion models. It works best on face swaps and fully synthetic faces.

Can I use this detector for videos?

Currently, we only support single image analysis. For videos, we recommend extracting frames and analyzing them individually. Video analysis is planned for a future release.

Is this technology really free?

Yes, completely free and open-source. We believe fighting misinformation should be accessible to everyone. You can find our code on GitHub and even contribute to the project.
